About Elisha Bay

Welcome to Elisha Bay, your trusted source for authentic Dagupan seafood and bangus dishes. We are dedicated to bringing the rich culinary traditions of Dagupan directly to your table, ensuring every meal is a celebration of freshness, quality, and flavor. From our waters to your home, we handle each product with care and expertise, guaranteeing that you receive only the finest seafood available.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for sharing the rich culinary heritage of Dagupan, Elisha Bay was born from a simple yet powerful mission: to make authentic, premium-quality seafood accessible to everyone. Our journey began in the heart of Dagupan City, where bangus (milkfish) is not just a dish—it's a way of life. Through years of dedication and commitment to excellence, we've grown from a small family operation into a trusted name in seafood delivery. Today, we continue to honor traditional preparation methods while embracing modern food safety standards, ensuring that every product we deliver captures the authentic taste of Dagupan that our customers have come to love and trust.
